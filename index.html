About Me


My name is Drew Norris, I am a full stack developer with a background in live video production. I am passionate about collaborating on technical and creative teams to build useful, beautiful web applications.

I just earned a certificate from UC Berkeley Extension Coding Bootcamp, emphasizing on the MERN stack. This was an intense, life-changing experience that not only taught me the skills to become a web developer but allowed me to flex my existing project management skills during the group projects. The course covered HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, JavaScript, JQuery, Node.js, Express.js, React.js, MySQL, MongoDB.

I am currently freelancing and working on some passion projects and am active in several coding groups both on and offline.
